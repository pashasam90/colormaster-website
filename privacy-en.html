Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

ColorMaster Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and profile name
  • Client Data: Client names, contact information, consultation records, and coloring history
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and frequency of use
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To synchronize data across your devices using cloud storage
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important notifications about the service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication
  • Data is stored in Firebase Firestore with access controls
  • Only authenticated users can access their own data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., Firebase, Apple)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@colormaster.app

11. International Users

If you are using our app from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred across international boundaries. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.
